ABOUT ME

Loretta

My journey of exploring plant-based diet began as a flexitarian a few years ago when I developed a desire
to become more conscious about food and its impact on my health and the environment. Today, I have
adopted a vegan lifestyle and my health has improved exponentially whereby I benefit from well being,
weight loss and healthier skin.

I am passionate about culinary art, healthy eating, and exploring new recipes from different cultures. My
passion for culinary art and healthy eating has enabled me to become creative to explore new ingredients,
food and recipes from across the world.

This blog and along with my ebook document plant- based recipes I have developed over several years. The
purpose of this eBook and this blog is to share the plant-based recipes, my knowledge about healthy eating
and nutrition.

The recipes I share in this blog are relatively simple and easy to make and I would highly encourage you to
commit to trying them out. I would love to hear feedback about the recipes once you try them.

If you like my blog and eBook, then share it with your friends and family members.
